About

Details

The D.Tech Puzzle Night is an exploration-focused math night for middle schoolers in the Bay Area. This event features a variety of interactive puzzle stations, each offering a different way to explore the fun side of mathematics. Students can try AMC-style math challenge problems, explore creative math modeling activities, and work through hands-on puzzle stations that highlight logic, problem-solving, and discovery. With activities for all levels, we encourage students to explore at their own pace, find what excites them, and build confidence in their problem-solving skills.

Activities and Stations

We are offering the following activities and stations:

Stations:

  • Pattern Recognition and Sequences
  • Word Problems and Riddles
  • Math Logic Puzzles
  • Math Crossword Puzzles
  • Grid-Based Deduction Puzzles (Minesweepers, Sudoku, Path-Finding)
  • Math Modeling Activities
  • Tessellations and Spatial Reasoning
  • Math Kangaroo Problems
  • AMC Style Problems

Events:

  • Countdown Round
  • Estimation

Please note that these stations and events are subject to change. Finalized details, including the Puzzle Night schedule, will be posted on our website and emailed closer to the competition date.

Sponsors

The D.Tech Math Club and D.Tech Puzzle Night are proudly sponsored by the Art of Problem Solving (AoPS), a leading organization dedicated to fostering problem-solving excellence and a lifelong passion for mathematics.

Since its founding, AoPS has empowered countless students worldwide to explore mathematics beyond the classroom through innovative textbooks, online courses, and vibrant math communities. Their support helps our club provide engaging opportunities for D.Tech students to collaborate, compete, and grow as mathematical thinkers. Learn more at https://artofproblemsolving.com.
