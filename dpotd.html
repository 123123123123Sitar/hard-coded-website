D.Tech Problem of the Day Challenge

The D.Tech Math Club hosts an online Problems of the Day Challenge, d.PotD, for middle school students in the week before DTMT. Over five days, students receive three problems each day, including one proof-based question, and have two hours to solve them. Spanning multiple subjects such as algebra, geometry, number theory, and combinatorics, these problems challenge reasoning and proof-writing skills while serving as a warm-up to DTMT.
