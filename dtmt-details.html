D.Tech Math Tournament (DTMT)

Friday in March, date and time is TBD | Design Tech High School

The D.Tech Math Tournament is a competitive yet welcoming event for middle school students, featuring various team and individual rounds, with creative problems in algebra, number theory, probability, combinatorics, and geometry that reward clever thinking and careful work.

About

Enhance problem-solving skills through fun activities.

This year's DTMT features an exciting and varied lineup of math challenges. Students will begin by selecting two individual tests from a set of topics: Algebra, Geometry, Combinatorics, Discrete Math, and a specially designed 6th Grade Test. Next, participants will team up for two collaborative rounds: the Sequence Round and the Team Round, where strategic thinking and teamwork take center stage. After the competition, students can take part in an activities session featuring Estimation, Puzzles, and Math Modeling‚Äîor enjoy a well-earned break with games and origami. At the same time, any tiebreakers will be resolved in a fast-paced, Mathcounts-style Countdown Round. The tournament will conclude with a Closing Ceremony where winners and prizes will be announced. DTMT will run from 4:30 to 9:30. Dinner is provided, and a $10 fee will be charged to help support our event. A detailed schedule will be published closer to the competition date.

Competition Structure

This year's DTMT features the following rounds and activities:

Individual Rounds:

  • Students select 2 tests from: Algebra, Geometry, Combinatorics, Discrete Math, and 6th Grade Test
  • Each test contains challenging problems designed to test deep understanding
  • Timed individual work promoting focus and problem-solving skills

Team Rounds:

  • Sequence Round: Teams work together to identify patterns and solve sequential problems
  • Team Round: Collaborative problem-solving requiring strategic thinking and communication

Activities & Events:

  • Estimation challenges
  • Logic puzzles and brain teasers
  • Math modeling activities
  • Games and origami during break time
  • Mathcounts-style Countdown Round for tiebreakers

Details:

  • Time: 4:30 PM to 9:30 PM
  • Registration fee: $10
  • Dinner provided
  • Open to middle school students in the Bay Area
  • Prizes and recognition at closing ceremony

Please note that the detailed schedule and specific round timings will be published closer to the competition date. All participants will receive updated information via email.

Sponsors

The D.Tech Math Club and D.Tech Math Tournament are proudly sponsored by the Art of Problem Solving (AoPS), a leading organization dedicated to fostering problem-solving excellence and a lifelong passion for mathematics.

Since its founding, AoPS has empowered countless students worldwide to explore mathematics beyond the classroom through innovative textbooks, online courses, and vibrant math communities. Their support helps our club provide engaging opportunities for students to collaborate, compete, and grow as mathematical thinkers. Learn more at https://artofproblemsolving.com.
